About Us

Urban Haven Apartments, located in the serene area of Murang'a, Kiharu, has been a trusted name in the house renting business for over 12 years. Since its inception, Urban Haven has built a reputation for providing quality and affordable housing options to the local community. With a focus on customer satisfaction and reliable service, the apartments have grown in popularity, becoming a top choice for individuals and families seeking a comfortable and convenient living space. As the housing industry evolves, Urban Haven Apartments recognizes the need to expand its reach and tap into the growing online market. With more customers turning to the internet to search for rental properties, Urban Haven is taking the initiative to establish an online presence. The first step in this exciting journey is to develop a professional and user-friendly landing page that reflects the values and services offered by the apartments.
